In Brief

"Born and raised in Lahore. From a business family with education and value addition for the community in its DNA."

— Iftikhar Hussain

Iftikhar Hussain is a multifaceted individual with an extraordinarily dynamic career spanning more than fourteen years. His core education is in Computer Sciences, but his passion for activism through social media and his interest in social entrepreneurship took root at university and has only grown since.

He is a power user of social media whose voice is not only heard but taken seriously, built on the ethical reputation of over a decade. His career has been rightly aligned with his personal mission of helping startups and the youth of Pakistan while establishing the right connections in the global landscape.

As Founder & CEO of Transforming Hub (2020 to 2024) he curated the award winning BIZNET 2022, 2023 and 2024, a premium gathering of startup founders, investors and the diplomatic community at the President House, Islamabad. It was the first time in the 125+ country history of Startup Grind that a sitting president of a country attended the event. He also organized two policy dialogues on diversity and financial inclusion with the Honorable President of Pakistan.

Today he serves as Director Growth Marketing & Startups, designing data driven growth strategies, optimising acquisition channels, mentoring startups across incubators, and curating high impact content. Earlier roles include Consultant and Head of Transformation & External Relations at Euro Petroleum, and Head of Global Business Development & Partnerships at OZI Group, where he expanded the group into the UAE and USA, transitioned it into electric vehicles, and signed the US Embassy Islamabad Green Alliance Framework.

As consultant to the Government of Punjab for the Women Development Department and the Women Innovation Network (a part of a £37.5 Million GBP DFID funded project), he launched a digital magazine, ran 360 marketing for Pakistan's first government female incubator, and worked on a PKR 3000 million Financial Inclusion through Mobile Wallet project for women in rural areas.

He is an IVLP Alumnus of the U.S. Department of State (Activism through Social Media, March 2014), a certified Trainer and Facilitator from the British Council, and the founder of DIGIMARK, Pakistan's premium digital marketing conference, with 6 iterations bringing 95+ C-level speakers from 80+ organizations and 4000+ corporate leaders.

He has been recognized by the President of Pakistan for grassroots community work to empower women, by the US Consul General Lahore William K. Manakole for contributions to PUAN Central Punjab, and by the Governor of Punjab for his services to the Marketing Association of Pakistan.
